SF-Muni

SF Muni

Using d3.js and Angular.js to draw the real-time positions of San Francisco's buses and trains.

The map will update the info every 15 seconds. And display the locations of buses in the last 15min.

Users can hover the routes on map to get the name of those routes.

Users can choose the routes from the left side-navigation, and see the highlighted routes and their associated buses on the map.

Developed by Lian Liu
